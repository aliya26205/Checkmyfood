Empowering You to Make Smarter Food Choices

We believe that everyone deserves to know what's in their food and whether it's safe to eat.
But, with the vast array of products on the market, it can be overwhelming to navigate the complex world of food labels and ingredients. That's why we created CheckMyFood - to give consumers the power to make informed choices about the food they eat.



How it Works


Our website uses advanced barcode scanning technology to quickly and accurately identify food products. We then retrieve product data from Open Food Facts, a comprehensive and trusted database of food products. Our proprietary algorithm analyzes the ingredients, allergens, and nutritional content of each product to calculate a safety percentage. This percentage gives users a clear and concise indication of the product's safety.
